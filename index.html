About

Persona is a non-profit organization that provides a platform for youth across the globe to build and develop leadership qualities. The platform provides them with the opportunity to connect with other young minds across the globe, organizing events and workshops. It also offers them courses and training to develop their skills and personality, helping them find their true potential. It provides the enthusiastic and talented ones with career opportunities.
